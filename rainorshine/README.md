# 🌦 Rain or Shine

This is Rain or Shine, a simple weather aggregator — because weather sucks. I just wanted to add a bit of shine to my rain, or more shine to my shine.

It pulls from 6 different weather sources and surfaces a consensus so you're not stuck trusting one app that swore it wouldn't rain and then absolutely rained.

**[→ Open Rain or Shine](https://kellykim.mp4/rainorshine)**

You can download it as a PWA or just access it whenever you want from your desktop. Add a city, check the vibe, go outside (or don't).
